How to Use

Simple Application Process

Getting the best results from Tree Serum products is straightforward and easy for every farmer.

1

Identify Your Crop & Growth Stage

Select the right Tree Serum product based on your crop type (sugarcane, banana, vegetables, etc.) and the current stage of plant growth.

2

Follow Recommended Dosage

For drip irrigation products: use 5 to 10 liters per acre as per stage guidelines. For spray products: mix 2 to 2.5 ml per liter of water. Always follow the label instructions.

3

Apply at the Right Time

Apply during early morning or evening for foliar sprays. For root zone products, apply through drip irrigation for best absorption and maximum effectiveness.

4

Can Be Mixed with Other Treatments

Tree Serum Bio NK-24 and other compatible products can be used alongside organic biological medicines. Always consult our experts for combination advice.

5

Observe & Repeat as Needed

Monitor crop response after application. Repeat as per the crop's seasonal schedule. Contact our support team for personalized guidance at any stage.
